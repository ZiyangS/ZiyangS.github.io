FORTIS Lab

FORTIS stands for Foundations Of Robust Trustworthy Intelligent Systems. Derived from the Latin word "fortis," meaning strong or resilient, FORTIS Lab focuses on developing AI systems that are built for robustness, trustworthiness, and scalability. Motivated by challenges like out-of-distribution (OOD) detection and other real-world complexities, the lab aims to design efficient, adaptable AI systems capable of solving problems in domains such as security, finance, healthcare, and AI4Science.
