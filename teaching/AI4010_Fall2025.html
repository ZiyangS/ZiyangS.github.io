AI 4010/5010 — Foundation of Deep Learning

Fall 2025 · Ohio University

Course description:

This course introduces the foundational concepts and techniques of deep learning. It begins with a review of classic machine learning (ML) algorithms, followed by an introduction to multi-layer neural networks and the backpropagation algorithm. The course then explores advanced deep learning architectures including convolutional neural networks (CNNs), recurrent neural networks (RNNs), and deep generative models. Students will gain both theoretical understanding and practical experience through lectures and hands-on exercises.
